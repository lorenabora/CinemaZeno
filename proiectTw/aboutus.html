About Us

The concept came in after a long debate on the movies. The fonder group with diffenrent tastes in films categories decided to join forces and visions into a project called "Cinema Zeno"" The image started to get clearer day after day, until the plan was perfect.


We present you our dream come true and invite you to experience an unique movie in a rare cinematography. We have a total of 8 halls, within there are the VintageHall, Wonder Room, AeroHall, UltraExp, Classic and other three. Each hall can support a number between 200 and 300 guests, the exact number is specified in the description of each room. The prices may differ from a hall to another or to the type of movie you selected, but don't worry! We apply some discount for certains categories of people or special days.

For more details about pricing, check out the Prices list in the section with the same name.


VintageHall

Our VintageHall is designed to copy the atmosphere of an old cinematography. With a lot of red seats, cozy, but small, the black walls and the dirty-white screen, the old black and white movies are yet again brought back to life for the lovers of the retro movies. We insist in trying this hall for at least once in a lifetime, to experience a true moment frozen in time.

AeroHall

On the other hand, AeroHall makes a dream come true for the futuristic guests or the ones who want to feel the SF or action movies. With boxes incorporated in the walls, seats that move with each attack or brutal action in the film, you ARE part of it, entirely. Give it a shot!

Wonder Room

All the kids these days enjoy a great movie on a bigger screen than the one from home, but have you ever seen a room specialy designed for them? The seats are comfty and a little bit taller for them to see the screen better; the boxes are diligently set for the best experience, but protecting the hearing of the little ones. The parents can stay in the room with them or let them on the hands of our staff, so they can enjoy a more suitable movie in another of our rooms. The animations are from classics to modern productions, so don't worry, they won't miss a single story!


UltraExp

Of course, we wouldn't miss the perfect room for any kind of movie. Do you want to feel the adrenaline running throw your veins? Do you want to feel the heartbeat stop? Or maybe do you simply want to see more? If that's the case, our Ultra is a perfect match, with a wide, curvy screen and special effects on your seats and walls, you can easily eneter the atmosphere of the movie you chose. We dare you to try watching a horror movie in this room! Are you brave enough?

Classic

For the ones that want to enjoy a movie and the arrangaments don't hold so much, this is fit for you. Come with a bunch of friends, or maybe on a date. The art of movies was invented to altern the reality and to give you a breaf break from your own life. Come and watch your favourite movie in a cozy seat, where only the lines of the actors will reach your soul.
