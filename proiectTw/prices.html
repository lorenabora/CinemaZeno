Only one is not fun!

Maybe coming to watch a movie solo is not a great idea, or maybe you want to enjoy it with your friends. Don't worry! For a group of friends, we let you pay only 70% the price per person! The promotion is only available for a group of at least 3 people!

Family pack

We know that having kids is not an easy job and to let them have fun without destroying something is exhausting or expensive. That's the main reason why we included this option for any family with at least 1 child! Come with them and for the whole family you will pay only 65% the price from the total. Attention!: a child is considered a person with less than 18 y/o.

Movie-Date

All the couples came at least once on a movie-date! Don't miss the opportunity to choose us for such a special night! What do we offer? Two tickets and some snacks at your choise! What's the catch?
